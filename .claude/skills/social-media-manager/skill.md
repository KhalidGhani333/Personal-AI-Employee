# Social Media Manager Skill

Create, schedule, and manage social media posts across platforms.

## Metadata
- **Tier**: Silver
- **Priority**: Medium
- **Dependencies**: Approval Handler, Dashboard Updater
- **Triggers**: Scheduled posts, content requests

## Capabilities
- Draft LinkedIn posts
- Draft Twitter/X posts
- Draft Facebook/Instagram posts
- Schedule posts
- Track engagement
- Generate content ideas
- Analyze post performance

## Usage
```bash
/social-media-manager --platform={linkedin|twitter|facebook} --action={draft|schedule|analyze}
```

## Expected Input
- Content topic or theme
- Target platform
- Scheduling preferences
- Business goals from Company_Handbook.md

## Expected Output
- Drafted posts in /Pending_Approval
- Scheduled posts calendar
- Engagement reports
- Content suggestions
